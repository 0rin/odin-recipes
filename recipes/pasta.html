Alfredo Pasta

A classic Italian-American favorite, alfredo pasta is easy to make at home with simple ingredients.

    INGREDIENTS
  • Fettucinne Pasta (16 oz)
  • Butter
  • Alfredo sauce of choice (sue me I'm not authentic)
    STEPS
  1. Bring 1 liter of water to a boil
  2. Boil fettucinne for 12 minutes, stirring occasionally
  3. Strain pasta, put back in pot and get saucy.
  4. Bone Apple Teeth
