Chili

Description

A spicy, highly seasoned food that may appear similar to a hearty soup at times. Chili is typically made with ground turkey, beef or pork, kidney beans, tomatoes, chiles, and seasonings all cooked in the broth of meat included in the Chili. Available in almost every food store, Chili is made with or without beans and with or without meat.

    INGREDIENTS
  • Ground Beef (16 oz.)
  • Sweet Onion
  • Green Pepper
  • Garlic Clove
  • Hot Chili Beans (1 can)
  • Tomato Paste (1 can)
  • Beef Broth (1/2-1 cup)
  • Chili Powder
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Powder
  • Crushed Black Pepper
  • Shredded Cheese of preference
  • Sour Cream
  • 2 TBSP Butter
    STEPS
  1. Chop sweet onion, green pepper and at least one clove of garlic
  2. Heat pot with 2 TBSP Butter and a dash of olive oil
  3. Begin to sautee onion and peppers
  4. Add ground beef to the pot
  5. Mix chili powder, paprika, garlic powder and ground pepper into pot
  6. Add minced garlic to pot
  7. Stir pot until ground beef begins to brown
  8. Add in can of chili beans and tomato paste and stir
  9. Add 1/2-1 cup beef broth to the pot, stir to mix
  10. Set heat to simmer, cook for 20-25 minutes
  11. Let chili sit for 10 minutes, scoop and serve with cheese and sour cream to your preference
